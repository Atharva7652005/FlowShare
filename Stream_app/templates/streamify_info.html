About Streamify

Connecting the world through seamless, high-quality video & file sharing.

Our Mission

At Streamify, we believe distance shouldn't limit connection. Our mission is to provide an ultra-low latency, high-definition video conferencing platform that just works. Whether for business meetings, casual catch-ups, or large-scale webinars, Streamify empowers you to communicate without barriers.

Key Features

  • Instant Meetings: Create or join rooms in seconds. No complex setups.
  • Crystal Clear HD: Optimized video compression for the best visual experience.
  • Secure Drops: Share files securely with our temporary, encrypted file drop rooms.
  • Privacy First: We don't store your video streams. What happens in the room, stays in the room.

Technology Stack

Built using the robust Django framework and modern WebRTC protocols, Streamify ensures reliability and scalability. Our frontend is powered by vanilla JavaScript and highly optimized CSS for a lightweight, fast-loading user interface.
